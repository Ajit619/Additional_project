About Spark Bank

Spark bank is the country's largest commercial bank it was established in 19 century in various parts of india.

Spark bank has over 2400 branches in India.In the financial year 2012–13, its revenue was ₹2.005 billion (US$28 million), out of which domestic operations contributed to 95.35% of revenue. Similarly, domestic operations contributed to 88.37% of total profits for the same financial year

In 1999, it became the first Indian company and the first bank or financial institution from non-Japan Asia to be listed on the New York Stock Exchange.

Spark Bank offers a wide range of banking products and financial services to corporate and retail customers through a variety of delivery channels and through its group companies.


Spark Bank is deeply engaged in human and economic development at the national level. The Bank works closely
with Government Foundation across diverse sectors and programs.
